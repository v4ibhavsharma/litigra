Suvineet Sharma has been in practice since 1977. He has twice served as Additional Advocate General for the State of Haryana. Over nearly five decades at the bar, he has handled matters across criminal, civil, and constitutional law before the Punjab and Haryana High Court.

His experience spans trial and appellate proceedings, including criminal defence, civil disputes, and writ proceedings. His sustained practice across multiple areas of law gives the firm access to a depth of courtroom experience that only an extended career at the bar can produce.
