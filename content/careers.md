<div class="careers-intro">

Litigra recruits advocates, interns, and paralegal staff at all levels. The firm handles a demanding litigation practice across the Supreme Court of India, the Punjab and Haryana High Court, the NCLT, the NCLAT, and other tribunals. Our work spans civil, criminal, corporate, insolvency, service, and tax law. The practice is built on careful preparation, plain drafting, and a professional culture that takes the work, and the people who do it, seriously.

We look for lawyers and interns who read carefully, write clearly, and are willing to engage with the substance of a matter rather than treating it as paperwork. A strong command of procedure, a willingness to work hard on court days, and a measured temperament count for more with us than grades.

</div>

<div class="careers-block">

### Advocates and Associates

We welcome applications from advocates at all levels of experience. Please send your curriculum vitae and a brief covering note describing your practice interests and the nature of the work you have handled so far.

</div>

<div class="careers-block">

### Internships

Law students from recognized institutions may apply for internships across the firm's practice areas. Internships are typically of four to six weeks. Applications should include a curriculum vitae, the proposed internship dates, and a short statement of interest.

</div>
