Adarsh Dubey is originally from Lucknow and practised at the Allahabad High Court before relocating to Chandigarh. His practice covers civil and constitutional litigation, with experience in writ proceedings and appellate matters.

His background at the Allahabad High Court has given him exposure to a range of civil, criminal, and constitutional matters across the courts of Uttar Pradesh.
