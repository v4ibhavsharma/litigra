Suvineet Sharma has been in practice since 1977. Over nearly five decades at the bar, he has handled matters across criminal, civil, and constitutional law before the Punjab & Haryana High Court.

His experience spans trial and appellate proceedings, and he has appeared in a wide range of matters including criminal defence, civil disputes, and writ proceedings. He brings a depth of courtroom experience that comes from sustained practice across multiple areas of law over an extended career.
