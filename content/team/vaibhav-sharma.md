Vaibhav Sharma has over 16 years of experience across corporate, commercial, and litigation matters. He holds a Master of Laws degree from the Army Institute of Law, Mohali.

From 2013 to 2017, he served as Deputy Advocate General for the State of Punjab, representing the state in matters before the Supreme Court and the Punjab & Haryana High Court. His work in that role covered writs, taxation, criminal, and service law. From 2020 to 2022, he served as Standing Counsel for the Comptroller and Auditor General of India in Haryana. He is an Empanelled Counsel for the Punjab State Agricultural Marketing Board and is also impanelled as counsel before the Punjab & Haryana High Court.

His practice covers civil and criminal litigation, corporate disputes, taxation, intellectual property, and family law. He has particular experience in mergers and acquisitions, insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings under the IBC, and disputes involving oppression and mismanagement before the National Company Law Tribunal. His exposure to a range of legal disciplines allows him to identify intersections between different areas of law that may affect a client's position.

He maintains an interest in legal technology and the application of artificial intelligence to legal practice. In 2012, he was included in Forbes' list of top ten Power Influencers in Mobile worldwide.
