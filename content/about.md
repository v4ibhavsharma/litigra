## ABOUT

Litigra is a litigation and advisory practice based in Chandigarh. The firm was established to bring together experienced practitioners across dispute resolution and legal advisory work. The name is derived from 'litigation' and 'grail', reflecting the firm's commitment to thorough and principled advocacy.

## PRACTICE

The firm handles matters across civil, commercial, corporate, constitutional, and criminal law. Areas of particular depth include corporate disputes, regulatory proceedings, commercial arbitration, and appellate practice before the Supreme Court and High Courts.

## APPROACH

Each matter is handled according to its specific requirements. The firm invests in understanding the facts, the applicable law, and the client's objectives before formulating a strategy. This applies equally to courtroom advocacy and advisory work.

## TEAM

The firm's practitioners bring experience from government service, corporate practice, and courtroom advocacy. This range of backgrounds allows the firm to approach matters from multiple perspectives.
