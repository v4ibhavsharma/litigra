Adarsh Dubey practised at the Allahabad High Court before relocating to Chandigarh. His practice covers civil and constitutional litigation, with experience in writ proceedings and appellate matters.

His background at the Allahabad High Court has given him exposure to a broad range of civil, criminal, and constitutional matters across the courts of Uttar Pradesh. He brings that breadth of experience to the firm's practice across jurisdictions.
